Executive Summary

Leruleng Mining Services (Pty) Ltd is a locally owned, community-based earthmoving business established to support Valterra Mogalakwena Mine and its contractors. We provide specialized earthmoving solutions including 30-ton excavators, 20-ton motor graders, TLB backhoe loaders — essential for road maintenance, bulk earthworks, trenching, rehabilitation, and general construction activities across the life of mine.

Our mission is to deliver reliable, safe, and productive equipment hire with operator model, creating sustainable local employment and technical capacity within Mapela, Mokopane, and surrounding villages.

Business Model Excellence

We operate a flexible equipment hire with operator model – offering hourly hire, daily hire, monthly contract hire, and long-term earthworks packages. All operations include:
