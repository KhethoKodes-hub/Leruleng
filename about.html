Executive Summary

Locally owned.
Community powered.

Leruleng Mining Services (Pty) Ltd is a community-based earthmoving company established to serve Valterra Mogalakwena Mine and its contractors.

We provide specialized earthmoving solutions — excavators, motor graders, and TLBs — essential for road maintenance, bulk earthworks, trenching, rehabilitation, and construction activities across the full life of mine. Our mission is to deliver reliable, safe, and productive equipment hire while creating sustainable local employment and technical capacity within Mapela, Mokopane, and surrounding villages.

  • Road construction, grading & haul road maintenance
  • Bulk earthworks & trenching operations
  • Environmental rehabilitation & compliance works
  • Foundations, drainage & civil infrastructure
  • Certified, accredited operators on every shift
