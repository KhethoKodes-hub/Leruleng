Market & Impact

Creating value for Mogalakwena

Market Opportunity

Mogalakwena Mine requires continuous earthmoving support across its full life of mine. Local, reliable suppliers with trained operators remain scarce — creating a compelling entry point for Leruleng Mining Services.

  • Road construction & haul road regrading
  • Bulk earthworks & underground trenching
  • Foundation construction & drainage works
  • Environmental compliance rehabilitation
  • Contractor support across all mine phases
